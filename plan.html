Goal

The primary goal of the 100SMILES Dental Care website is to increase new patient bookings by 30% within six months of launch and to establish the practice as the trusted, family-friendly dental provider in the Razole community. This will be achieved by providing a clear, reassuring, and easy-to-use online presence that simplifies the process of learning about services, meeting the staff, and booking an appointment, thereby converting website visitors into patients.

Success Evaluation

Success will be measured using the following quantifiable metrics:

1. Conversion Tracking: Monitoring the number of online appointment requests submitted through the website's contact form or clicks on the "Call to Book" phone number link.

2. Promotion Uptake: Tracking the usage of the "20% discount on your first visit" offer by requiring patients to mention the "website offer" when booking.

3. User Engagement: Using analytics to measure time spent on key pages (Services, Staff), low bounce rates, and clicks on the informational articles, indicating that users are finding the site informative and trustworthy.

Target Audience

1. Target Audience Description:
The primary target audience is families and adults aged 25-50 living in and around Razole, QLD. This group is typically responsible for their own and their children's healthcare decisions, values trust and long-term relationships with service providers, and uses the internet to research local businesses. A secondary audience is seniors and retirees in the area who may require more complex dental work and value experienced, compassionate care.

2. Intentional Design Approach:
The site will be intentionally designed for this audience by:
- Visual Design: Using a clean, professional, and calming colour palette (e.g., blues, whites, soft greens) with high-quality images of the staff and clinic to build immediate trust and familiarity. The design will feel modern yet warm and approachable.
- Content & Tone: The language will be reassuring, compassionate, and free of overly complex medical jargon. It will emphasize key family-friendly values like "for all ages," "preventative care," "friendly," and "compassionate."
- Functionality: The site structure will be simple and intuitive, with a clear path to booking an appointment. Key information like phone numbers, trading hours, and the first-visit discount will be easy to find, catering to busy parents and those who may be anxious about dental visits.
